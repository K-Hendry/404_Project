shiba land

Our dog park was built by Kelsey Hendry and Han Khuong as a part of a larger project to show appreciation to adorable doggos. Shiba Land has done amazing work with the city and nonprofit partners to not only improve our lives by building the ultimate place for puppies, but also to construct a nicely designed website for our Web Design class.

Visitors will definitely find themselves lost in a feeling of wholesomeness as they walk, bike or ride horses through this undisputed gem of Philadelphia’s park system.
